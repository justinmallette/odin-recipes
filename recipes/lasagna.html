Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce using your meat, onion and garlic, tomato products, and spices
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture using your cheeses, egg, and spices
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake at 375 degrees for 50 minutes
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
